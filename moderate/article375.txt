November 18, 2016, 8:14 AM | Three years ago, Time magazine began a massive project to pick the most influential photos ever, from the first photo of Earth from space to the world's most famous kiss. All 100 photos are featured in a new book and on an interactive website. Time editor-in-chief Nancy Gibbs and Kira Pollack, director of photography and visual enterprise, join "CBS This Morning" to share the stories behind some of the iconic photos.
