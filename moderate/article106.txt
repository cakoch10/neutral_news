March 25, 2017, 8:45 AM | Born in South Carolina, Marcus King was brought up on the blues, performing with his bluesman father, Marvin. Last year the band released their critically-acclaimed second album produced by Warren Haynes of The Allman Brothers. The Marcus King Band performs "Ain't Nothin' Wrong With That" from their self-titled album.
